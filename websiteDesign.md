

# Beta Design

![Webpage Beta](/images/betaDesign.png)

## details
- Each sensor has its own distinct color so you never confuse them at a glance.
- The colors are warm/energetic and intuitively matching what they measure: heat, light, and water/humidity.
- Dark theme reduces eye strain and suits outdoor/low-light reading
Minimal UI which only job is to show three numbers clearly.
- Progress bar under each value meant to give the reading context about how high/low is it relative to its range.

## details
- The original color pallete was meant to be 6 colors, they are referenced in case other elements are added (pink - #FF006E, purple - #8338EC ). 
- Dark colors are meant to visually match the hardware enclosure the ESP32 and other componets sit on.
